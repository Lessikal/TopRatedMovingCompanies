As a moving broker, Safe Ship has access to a nationwide network of trusted movers and truckers to safely relocate any family from one state in the continental USA to another. Sporting low rates and lighting-fast estimates, Safe Ship is a great choice for any long distance move.

About Safe Ship

Committed to safety and affordability, Safe Ship is a licensed moving broker that specializes in the relocation of household goods. Safe Ship offers interstate (i.e. from one state to the next) and long distance moving services for residential families, military families, and corporate offices.

Safe Ship’s Moving Services

Through its network of moving professionals and affiliates, Safe Ship offers full-service moving across interstate lines. When we say “full service,” we mean that Safe Ship will do all of the work involved with a move: boxing up belongings, packing, transporting, unpacking, and unboxing.

Moving with Safe Ship

Here’s what your relocation journey will look like with help from Safe Ship:

  • 1. Estimate: Enter your moving details into Safe Ship’s easy-to-use online estimator to receive a near-instant, ballpark estimate.
  • 2. Plan: Lay out the details of your move: what is being moved, where you are moving to, what day and time you’re moving, etc.
  • 3. Confirm: Safe Ship will send over a representative to make an in-person estimate of your belongings. A representative will reach out within a day or two of your estimate to agree on a contract.
  • 4. Pack: Organize and pack your belongings by yourself or with support from Safe Ship affiliates.
  • 5. Load and Travel: Safe Ship employees or affiliates load your belongings onto a company truck and haul your goods to your new location.
  • 6. Unload and Unpack: Safe Ship employees or affiliates unload your boxes for you and even help you unpack if you request.

Pricing

Long distance moving assistance from Safe Ship starts at $1,500. Typically, $1,500 is not quite enough money to cover a rental truck for DIY packing and hauling, making Safe Ship a great value for some moves.

That said, every move is priced differently. Each part of your relocation journey will affect your price in a unique way, including your number of belongings, distance moved, and time frame required.

For the most accurate pricing possible, we recommend you start by getting a free estimate from Safe Ship’s website.

Do you have more questions about how professional moves are priced? Check out our detailed article “9 Tips for Choosing a Long Distance Moving Company" for all the essentials.

The Bottom Line

Thanks to official licensing from the US Department of Transportation, low rates, and relocation services for everyday families or corporate offices, we highly recommend Safe Ship Moving Services for your next long distance move.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How Early Should I Book a Professional Mover?
The golden rule of booking a professional move is to book ASAP. If you’re in a bit of a pinch, most interstate moves can be booked two or three months in advance. For international moves, you should book at least six months in advance.
How Do I Choose the Right Moving Company?
The easiest way to decide on a moving company is to break the process into two steps. First, click through the moving companies included on our list and see which appeal to your relocation needs. Secondly, get and compare quotes from your list of favorite companies. After comparing quotes, you’ll see which company offers you the best deal for your situation.
Where Can I Move to with a Professional Mover?
The moving companies on our list will relocate customers from one state to another, or from the United States to another country.
