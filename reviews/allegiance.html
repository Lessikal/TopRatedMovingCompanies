Allegiance Moving and Storage: Review

Allegiance Moving and Storage is a licensed relocation broker that serves customers in all 50 states. Allegiance offers full-service relocation for interstate, residential moves. In addition to moving, Allegiance offers short- and long-term storage solutions, great for adding additional flexibility to the moving process.

With over 20 years of experience, Allegiance will get you where you need to go.

About Allegiance

From Hawaii to New York, Allegiance can move you from one state to any other state thanks to their nationwide network of moving affiliates.

Allegiance excels by being flexible to each customer’s unique moving needs. Do you need to go into your old office for two weeks after your belongings are shipped out? No problem, Allegiance will store your belongings for free, for up to 30 days. Do you have to relocate your entire office to your parent company’s city, which is located across the country? No need to lift a finger: Allegiance can move your office supplies for you.

Moving with Allegiance

Getting serious about moving with Allegiance? Here’s what the process will look like:

  • 1. Estimation: Enter your moving details into  Allegiance’s online estimator and see a price range for your upcoming move.
  • 2. Logistical Planning: Allegiance helps you plan each and every step of your move.
  • 3. Contract Agreement: Allegiance will send over a representative to make an in-person estimate of your belongings. Once a contract is written, it’s up to you to agree or push back on the price before you sign it.
  • 4. Packing: Allegiance affiliates organize, pack, and box up your belongings.
  • 5. Transportation: Allegiance affiliates transport your belongings through company trucks or freighters.
  • 6. Unloading: Allegiance affiliates unpack and unpack your belongings in your new home. Alternatively, the team can unpack your belongings into a storage unit.

Pricing

To get a ballpark estimation of your moving costs,  visit Allegiance’s website and request a quote.

For the most accurate pricing possible, you’ll need to bring in an Allegiance representative to appraise your belongings in person.

For a detailed description of how professional moving companies and brokers price your move, check out our article “9 Tips for Choosing a Long Distance Moving Company.”

The Bottom Line

In addition to offering licensed interstate moving for residential and commercial spaces, Allegiance has a suite of helpful services at its fingertips. Allegiance can transport your vehicle, pack and unpack your belongings, and store your belongings near your new home (for up to 30 days free).

If you’re looking for the security of a professional moving company with the flexibility of a nationwide broker, look no further than Allegiance Moving and Storage.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How Early Should I Book a Professional Mover?
The golden rule of booking a professional move is to book ASAP. If you’re in a bit of a pinch, most interstate moves can be booked two or three months in advance. For international moves, you should book at least six months in advance.
How Do I Choose the Right Moving Company?
The easiest way to decide on a moving company is to break the process into two steps. First, click through the moving companies included on our list and see which appeal to your relocation needs. Secondly, get and compare quotes from your list of favorite companies. After comparing quotes, you’ll see which company offers you the best deal for your situation.
Where Can I Move to with a Professional Mover?
The moving companies on our list will relocate customers from one state to another, or from the United States to another country.
Are Professional Moving Companies Insured in the Case of an Incident?
Most of the moving companies on our list are insured. Please see the company websites listed above for more information.
